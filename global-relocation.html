Relocation

No matter where globally you are relocating, SSBC understands that the process involves a lot more than simply transporting items from point A to point B. After-all we have been safely moving precious belongings around the world. SSBC offers a comprehensive range of professional services to anyone moving overseas.If you want to move overseas with complete peace of mind, and enjoy a complete door-to-door service from an experienced and professional team, then contact us today.

1. PROMPT, SECURE AND ON TIME DELIVERY

Our advanced tracking and management systems will ensure we have a full inventory of all your belongings, and we’ll know where everything is at all times. Your goods will arrive where you expect, when you expect them, and in the same condition they were in before the move. Since we offer both air and sea shipping, we can handle virtually any size move – from a studio apartment, to a large estate.

2. PROFESSIONAL PACKING SERVICES

Our professional packers provide a superior service, so you can sit back, relax and just leave this job to us. Whether you want us to pack your entire household or just certain selected items, we will arrive at your residence and get the job done. Our staff is experienced and have the right expertise in packing goods for transport, shipping and storage; protecting your belongings from any kind of damage during the move overseas.

3. YOUR TOTAL MOVING SERVICE

Contact us before you start making your arrangements, and you’ll be pleasantly surprised at how much of the process we can handle. We would gladly help plan and manage your move overseas. Our experienced moving consultants will cover off all aspects of your move, even things you may not have thought of or anticipated. Then let us take away the hassle and stress by managing all the details, including professional packing, safe and secure transport of all your household treasures, as well as customs clearance.
